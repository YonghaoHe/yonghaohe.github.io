Yonghao He

Director of Embodied AI @ D-Robotics

I am currently leading R&D in embodied AI at D-Robotics, covering VLA/VA models, Real2Sim2Real, data collection infrastructure, 3D vision foundation models, and high-precision manipulation solutions for industrial scenarios.

I have long engaged with the frontier of artificial intelligence both as an observer and as a practitioner. Since the onset of the current wave of AI (from around 2012), I have witnessed the evolution of the technology and the industry, through to today’s flourishing entrepreneurial ecosystem driven by AI.

As a co-founder or core team member, I have helped build multiple AI-related companies across domains including security, industrial vision, autonomous driving, and, most recently, embodied AI. This experience has given me a systematic understanding of how AI advances and how industries evolve; I am adept at connecting cutting-edge research with productization and at quickly grasping the essence of complex problems.

What I Believe in

  • Embodied AI will connect the physical and digital worlds; AGI will emerge within our generation.
  • In any era, representation learning deserves deep study—it is the most foundational problem.
  • The world’s demand for AI has only begun to penetrate (~1%); the landscape for AI inference chips is far from its final form.
